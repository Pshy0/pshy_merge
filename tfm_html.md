# TFM custom markups

- `<bv>`: dark cyan (map crew color)
- `<bl>`: dark blue (bleu, system messages color)
- `<ch>`: light cyan (chamane)
- `<ch2>`: light pink (chamane 2)
- `<d>`: light yellow
- `<g>`: gray (gris, offline color)
- `<fc>`: orange (funcorp)
- `<j>`: yellow
- `<n>`: white (neige, regular chat)
- `<o>`: orange
- `<r>`: red (rouge)
- `<rose>`: pink
- `<t>`: light green (tribe chat color)
- `<v>`: green (chat names)
- `<vp>`: lime (tutorial)
- `<vi>`: purple
